WARNER BROS

Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc. is a global media and entertainment company that produces and distributes feature films, television programming, interactive entertainment, music, and licensed merchandise. The company is known for producing some of the most iconic and successful films in history, including The Wizard of Oz, Casablanca, the Harry Potter series, and The Dark Knight Trilogy. Warner Bros. is also home to DC Entertainment, which produces films and television shows based on DC Comics characters, and HBO, which produces critically acclaimed television programming. Founded in 1923, Warner Bros. is headquartered in Burbank, California and is a subsidiary of AT&T's WarnerMedia.
